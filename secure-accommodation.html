Tips When Looking for Off-Campus Housing

  • If possible, arrive 2 weeks to a month before classes start to arrange off-campus housing.
  • Visit the accommodation in person or ask for detailed photos, or have a video call tour.
  • Meet or ask about roommates.
  • Read rental agreement details carefully.
  • Be informed: Does the rent include utilities and laundry facilities? How close is it to public transportation, groceries, or other services?
  • You are not required to disclose your SIN to your landlord or roommates.
