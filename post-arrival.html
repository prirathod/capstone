Post-Arrival Requirements

  • Settle into your accommodation: Unpack, arrange utilities, and get comfortable in your new space.
  • Register for necessary documents: Apply for your SIN, health card, and collect your university ID.
  • Explore transportation options: Understand local transit systems and secure passes or tickets.
  • Set up local banking and budgeting: Activate your Canadian bank account and manage your finances effectively.
  • Participate in orientation and support services: Attend university programs to learn about resources and make friends.
  • Seek employment opportunities: Explore part-time jobs or internships to gain work experience and support yourself financially.
  • Familiarize yourself with food, cooking, and social adjustment: Explore grocery stores, develop cooking skills, and adapt to local social norms.
