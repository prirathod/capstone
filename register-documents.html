A. Apply for Your Social Insurance Number (SIN)

A Social Insurance Number (SIN) is essential for working in Canada and accessing government services.

How to Apply:

  1. Online: Visit the Service Canada website and follow the instructions to apply online. You'll need to upload digital copies of your original documents.
  2. In-Person: Apply personally at a Service Canada Centre. Bring your original documents, such as your passport and study permit.

For the list of documents, please visit the Service Canada website.

PLEASE NOTE: A SIN is issued to one person only and cannot legally be used by anyone else. Protect your SIN – do not disclose it to anyone except government authorities or employers when required. Store documents containing your SIN in a safe place.

B. Apply for an Alberta Health Card

The Alberta Health Care Insurance Plan (AHCIP) provides residents of Alberta access to healthcare services.

How to Apply:

  1. Fill out the application form or download it from the Alberta Government website.
  2. Gather supporting documents:
    • Passport
    • Canada entry document (study or work permit)
    • Proof of Alberta residency, such as a stamped form from your bank or a utility bill with your full name and address dated within 90 days.
  3. Bring the completed application form and original documents to an authorized registry in your area. There is no charge for this service.

Processing time can take up to 5 days. Cards are mailed from the AHCIP office.

C. MacEwan University ID

Your University ID card is essential for accessing campus facilities, libraries, and other services at MacEwan University.

Request a New/Replacement ID:

  • Submit your photo via the MacEwan ID Photo Upload Form. You'll be contacted when the card is ready for pickup.
  • If you haven't received the pickup email, contact library@macewan.ca.
  • Don’t have a camera? Contact library@macewan.ca for assistance.

Lost Cards:

Report lost ID cards to the library immediately. Replacement fee is $10.
