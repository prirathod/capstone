What to Bring: (Must Have at the Airport)

  • A valid passport (and TRV or eTA if required)
  • Your Letter of Acceptance (LOA) or Term Enrollment and Account Summary for the upcoming term
  • Provincial Attestation Letter (PAL), if applicable
  • The letter received from the visa office when your Study Permit was approved (contains your Study Permit reference number)
  • Travel and health insurance

Other Documents to Secure

  • Original transcripts (educational documents)
  • Other official identifications (e.g., driver’s license)
  • Any medical records and medical prescriptions
  • References from previous employers or teachers if planning to seek employment in Canada

Helpful Tip

Keep all documents in a re-sealable folder in your carry-on or hand luggage for easy travel access. It is wise to scan these documents and save electronic copies, email them to yourself, or leave another set of documents with your family in case you lose the originals.
