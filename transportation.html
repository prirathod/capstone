Transportation Options from Edmonton International Airport (YEG) to Edmonton

Bus

Take Bus 747 from the airport to the Century Park bus and light rail transit (LRT) terminal in south Edmonton. At the terminal, you can either take another bus or the train to your destination.

View Bus Details

Taxi

Taxis are available 24/7. Look for a Yellow Cab on the curb at the airport. Check the rates using the guide linked below.

More Information on Taxi Services

Uber

Book an Uber to pick you up and drop you off right at your accommodation’s doorsteps.

Learn More About Uber in Edmonton

Pick Up

If you know someone in Edmonton who can pick you up at the airport, the passenger pick-up area is located on the lower ramp on the Arrivals level.
