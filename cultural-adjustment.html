1. Culture

  • Edmonton is diverse and welcoming, with many cultural events and festivals.
  • Canadians value politeness, punctuality, and respectful communication.

2. Customs

  • Tipping: It is common to tip 15-20% in restaurants.
  • Enjoy local foods like poutine and Alberta beef.
  • Explore festivals such as K-Days (summer) and Ice on Whyte (winter).

3. Climate (Edmonton)

  • Winter: Very cold, often below -20°C. Bring a warm coat, gloves, boots, and thermal clothing.
  • Summer: Warm and sunny, around 20-25°C.
  • Use layers to stay comfortable and moisturizers for dry air.
