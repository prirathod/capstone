Opening a Bank Account

You can open a bank account when you arrive in Canada. Alternatively, you can purchase a General Investment Certificate (GIC) before arriving.

What is a General Investment Certificate (GIC)?

  • A risk-free investment that international students can purchase to meet the financial requirements for studying in Canada.
  • Helps international students demonstrate that they can support themselves financially while studying in Canada.
  • The minimum investment amount is $20,635, as set by IRCC.
  • A specific amount is redeemed each month, including interest, to cover expenses.
