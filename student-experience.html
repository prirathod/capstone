Student Experience at MacEwan University

At MacEwan University, students are more than just learners; they are a part of a dynamic community focused on personal, academic, and professional growth. Below are some stories from students about how the university has helped them achieve their goals:

Sarah, Business Student

“MacEwan gave me the resources and support I needed to succeed. The Business program is practical and tailored to the real world. I was able to apply my learning during my internship and now work for one of Canada's top financial firms. Without the faculty's encouragement and support, I wouldn’t have reached my goals.”

James, Arts Student

“As an Arts student, I was given the freedom to explore my passion for graphic design. The professors were mentors who helped me hone my skills and develop a strong portfolio. I’ve now successfully launched my own freelance business. MacEwan’s networking opportunities were key to this achievement.”

Amy, Nursing Student

“Becoming a nurse has always been my dream, and MacEwan helped make that possible. The clinical placements allowed me to gain hands-on experience, and the faculty were always available to offer guidance and support. Thanks to their mentorship, I’m now working as a registered nurse in a hospital that I’ve always admired.”

Mark, Computer Science Student

“The Computer Science program at MacEwan pushed me to think outside the box. The collaborative environment and access to state-of-the-art technology helped me develop my skills in coding and problem-solving. I recently landed a software development job at a leading tech company, all thanks to the academic experience MacEwan provided.”

Lucy, Psychology Student

“MacEwan’s Psychology program opened my eyes to the complexities of human behavior. The hands-on experience in the lab, paired with supportive faculty members, gave me a deep understanding of the field. Today, I am pursuing a graduate degree, all thanks to the strong foundation MacEwan provided.”
